The Accidents Map allows you to see when and where 188 known petrochemical accidents occurred along the lower Schuylkill River, as superimposed on a current map. The date range is 1866 to 2019. Each dot or diamond represents one accident. Diamonds indicate nearly exact locations, whereas dots indicate approximate locations. Red indicates fatal; orange indicates injurious; and blue indicates non-injurious. Zoom in until you can distinguish individual accidents, then click on any dot or diamond to see associated information. Be aware that this map is optimized for computer screens, not mobile screens.
